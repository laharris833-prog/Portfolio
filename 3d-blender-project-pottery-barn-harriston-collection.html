3D Blender Project - Pottery Barn Harriston Collection

Lauren Harris
February 19, 2026
Updated on February 19, 2026

Developed a high-quality 3D environment in Blender to simulate a marketing campaign for the 'Harriston Collection' - an imaginative furniture collection based on the American brand, Pottery Barn. 

Software: Blender (Cycles Render Engine)

Techniques: PBR Texturing, Global Illumination, Virtual Staging

  • 2-Stage Lighting Enhancement: Applied post-render lighting passes to refine specular highlights and atmospheric depth.

  • 3-Stage Lighting Enhancement: Utilized a Key, Fill, and Rim light configuration in Blender Cycles to establish realistic volume and product separation.

Marketing Initiatives

Execution: Integrated custom-modeled 3D rendering into a high-end editorial layout, utilizing brand-specific typography and copywriting to mimic the Pottery Barn aesthetic.

Result: A consumer-facing advertisement that visually narrates the company's style through inimitable 3D furniture and room design.
